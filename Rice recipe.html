Brazilian White Rice

This is a simple and delicious recipe for traditional Brazilian white rice. It's simple yet tasty, seasoned with garlic and onion in a way that makes all the difference.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups of water

Instructions

  1. Peel the garlic and onion. Smash the garlic into a paste and finely chop the onion.
  2. Pour olive oil into a pan and sauté the onion first. When the onion is soft, add the garlic and cook until slightly browned, but not burned.
  3. Add the rice and stir continuously for about 2 minutes so it doesn’t burn.
  4. Add 3 cups of water and salt. Cover and cook on low heat until the water is absorbed and the rice is soft.
